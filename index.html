Featured Project

AIA4OneHealth

Machine Learning · Bayesian Modeling · Multi-Objective Optimization

An end-to-end machine learning pipeline that couples a Bayesian-calibrated mechanistic Mpox model with an ML emulator ensemble and NSGA-II multi-objective optimization to recommend One Health policy packages for Nigeria and the Democratic Republic of the Congo (DRC).

  • Mechanistic modeling: Coupled human–rodent SEIR-V ODE model
  • Bayesian calibration: MCMC with negative-binomial likelihood and posterior predictive checks
  • ML emulators: 5-model ensemble (GP-Matérn, GP-RBF, Random Forest, XGBoost, Neural Net)
  • Optimization: NSGA-II 3-objective Pareto front (cases × cost × cross-domain equity)
  • Forecasting: XGBoost lag-feature next-week case forecaster
  • Sensitivity analysis: Sobol' total-effect indices over One Health levers
